Automated prediction accuracy tracking across all leagues — simulated $100 flat bets

How the Model Tracker Works

  • Generate Tracker creates an entry for every prediction that doesn't have one yet.
  • Settle Results checks pending entries against actual match results and calculates P&L.
  • Every Game Strategy: We simulate betting $100 on the model's top pick for every match.
  • Value Bets Only Strategy: We only bet $100 when the model finds an edge of 5%+ over bookmaker odds.
  • P&L is calculated as: Win = $100 × odds − $100; Loss = −$100.
