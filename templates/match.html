Model Signals

{{ prediction.models_available }} models contributing

How to read each model card

Each card shows one model's independent prediction. w=25% means that model carries 25% weight in the final blended prediction.

H 63% = Home team has 63% chance of winning
D 18% = 18% chance of a draw
A 19% = Away team has 19% chance of winning

The four models

poisson w=35% — Based on goals scored & conceded. Generates scoreline probabilities.

xgboost w=30% — Machine learning on 20 features: form, H2H, rest days, referee, etc.

elo w=25% — Chess-style power rating updated after every match.

sentiment w=10% — Reddit & news mood. Weak signal but catches injuries/drama.

How the ensemble blends them

Final = (Poisson × 0.35) + (XGBoost × 0.30) + (Elo × 0.25) + (Sentiment × 0.10)

When models agree

All pointing the same way = higher confidence, stronger signal.

When models disagree

Elo says Home but Sentiment says Away = lower confidence, be cautious.

What do these signals mean?

Referee: Some referees consistently oversee higher or lower scoring games. The avg goals/game and Over 2.5 rate show this referee's tendencies.

Fixture Congestion: Teams playing many games in a short window tend to fatigue. More matches in 14 days = higher congestion risk.

Market Movement: When bookmaker odds shorten (decrease), money is backing that outcome. This shows how the market has moved since opening.

Half-Time Patterns: How each team's goals split between first and second halves. "Comeback" teams score more in the 2nd half; "Fast starters" score early.

Promoted Status: Newly promoted teams get an Elo adjustment. They often overperform early-season expectations.
