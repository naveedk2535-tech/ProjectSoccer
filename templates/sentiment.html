Sentiment Tracker — Help

What is this page?

This tracks what the internet thinks about each team. We scan Reddit (r/soccer, r/PremierLeague) and news headlines, then score the mood from -1 (very negative) to +1 (very positive).

Why does this matter?

Sentiment is a weak signal (only 10% of our prediction model), but it occasionally catches things the stats miss — like when a key player gets injured, a manager gets sacked, or there's drama in the dressing room. A sudden spike in volume (lots of posts) usually means something big happened.

How to read the cards

  • Score: -1 to +1. Above +0.1 = positive vibes. Below -0.1 = negative vibes.
  • Volume: How many Reddit posts mentioned the team this week. More = more attention.
  • Trend: Is sentiment getting better or worse vs last week?
