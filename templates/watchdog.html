What does the Watchdog do?

The Watchdog monitors your ProjectSoccer installation for potential issues across five categories: App Health (database connectivity, size, tables), Security (.env file, .gitignore, hardcoded keys, suspicious files), Data Integrity (row counts, sudden drops, league coverage), API Keys (presence and validity of required keys), and Model Health (XGBoost model existence, freshness, prediction quality). Each check returns pass, warn, or critical status. Critical issues trigger an alert flag.
