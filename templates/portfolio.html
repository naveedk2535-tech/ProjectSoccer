Bet Portfolio — Help

What is this page?

Your personal betting portfolio — think of it like a stock trading dashboard but for bets.

Portfolio Seasons

Create named portfolios (e.g. "Gameweek 28", "March 2026") to track bets in separate seasons. Each has its own bankroll, bets, and P&L. Use "All Time" to see cumulative stats across all portfolios. Close a portfolio when a season ends. Existing bets without a portfolio are shown in the All Time view.

Starting Bankroll

How much money you've set aside for betting. Click 'Edit' to change it. If you don't set it, we auto-calculate from your total wagers. Each portfolio can have its own bankroll.

How to use it

  1. Find value bets on the Command Center or match pages
  2. Use the Bet Calculator on any match page to enter your stake
  3. Click 'Add to Portfolio' to track the bet
  4. When the match is played, come here and click 'Won', 'Lost', or 'Void'
  5. Watch your P&L, win rate, and ROI build over time

What the numbers mean

  • Win Rate: % of settled bets you've won
  • ROI: Return on investment — profit as % of total staked
  • Kelly: The model's suggested stake size. We use quarter-Kelly (conservative)
  • Win Streak: How many wins in a row you're currently on
