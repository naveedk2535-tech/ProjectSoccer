How To Use ProjectSoccer

Reading the Predictions

Each match shows three percentages:

H

54%

D

21%

A

25%

H = Home team wins (54% chance)

D = Draw (21% chance)

A = Away team wins (25% chance)

The highlighted one (green/blue glow) is the model's pick. Click any match to see the full breakdown.

What's a Value Bet?

A value bet is when our model disagrees with the bookmaker -- and we think the bookmaker is wrong.

Example: Our model says Team X has a 35% chance to win.

The bookmaker offers odds of 4.00, which implies only a 25% chance.

That 10% gap is the edge. Over many bets, this edge = profit.

Edge % = How wrong we think the bookmaker is

Kelly % = Suggested stake as % of your total bankroll

How Winnings Work

Odds tell you what you'll get back if you win:

Odds of 1.85 = Bet $10, get back $18.50 ($8.50 profit)

Odds of 3.00 = Bet $10, get back $30.00 ($20.00 profit)

Odds of 6.80 = Bet $10, get back $68.00 ($58.00 profit)

Real example:

Tottenham vs Nottingham Forest

Our model: Forest wins 45% | Bookmaker: Forest wins 34% (odds 2.96)

You're being overpaid. Over 100 bets at $10:

Win ~45 x $19.60 profit = $882

Lose ~55 x $10 lost = $550

NET PROFIT: +$332 = +33% ROI

You WILL lose individual bets. The edge shows up over volume. You're the casino. Backtest: 348 bets, +31.3% ROI.

The Four Models — How They Work

1. Poisson + Dixon-Coles (35% weight)

Calculates each team's Attack Strength and Defence Weakness vs league average. Produces expected goals, then uses Poisson distribution for a 7x7 scoreline matrix. Dixon-Coles corrects for low-scoring games (0-0, 1-0, 1-1). Now includes second-half strength multiplier from goal timing analysis.

2. XGBoost Machine Learning (30% weight)

Gradient-boosted trees trained on 28 features: Elo, form, H2H record, attack/defence strength, days rest, referee tendencies, fixture congestion, market movement, Pinnacle implied probability, sentiment, month, promoted team status, streak length.

3. Elo Rating System (25% weight)

Chess-style power ratings with home advantage (+50), goal difference multiplier, season decay. Detects promoted teams and gives them +75 Elo boost for first 6 matches. Tracks home/away Elo separately, plus form and streaks.

4. Sentiment Analysis (10% weight)

Reddit + news headlines scored by VADER. Tracks volume spikes and flags keywords (injury, sacked, ban). Weak signal but catches things stats miss.

How the Ensemble Works

Final = (Poisson × 0.35) + (XGBoost × 0.30) + (Elo × 0.25) + (Sentiment × 0.10)

Weights auto-optimize weekly based on historical Brier scores. If a model fails, remaining weights re-normalise.

Kelly Criterion sizes bets: f = (bp - q) / b. We use quarter-Kelly (conservative).

Your Match Day Workflow

1. Morning — hit "Refresh Everything" (gets overnight news + fresh odds)

2. Check Value Bets across all leagues

3. Click into matches for the deep dive + prediction signals

4. Pre-match — hit Refresh again for latest odds movement

5. Place bets via the Bet Calculator, track in Portfolio

6. After matches — Refresh to get results, check Model Tracker

You can refresh 2x/day across all 3 leagues. The model finds VALUE, not winners. Trust the process over volume.
