How To Use ProjectSoccer

Reading the Predictions

Each match shows three percentages:

H

54%

D

21%

A

25%

H = Home team wins (54% chance)

D = Draw (21% chance)

A = Away team wins (25% chance)

The highlighted one (green/blue glow) is the model's pick. Click any match to see the full breakdown.

What's a Value Bet?

A value bet is when our model disagrees with the bookmaker -- and we think the bookmaker is wrong.

Example: Our model says Team X has a 35% chance to win.

The bookmaker offers odds of 4.00, which implies only a 25% chance.

That 10% gap is the edge. Over many bets, this edge = profit.

Edge % = How wrong we think the bookmaker is

Kelly % = Suggested stake as % of your total bankroll

The Four Models

1. Poisson + Dixon-Coles (35% weight)

Statistical goal-scoring model using attack/defence strengths and scoreline probability matrices.

2. XGBoost Machine Learning (30% weight)

Gradient-boosted trees trained on 20 features including Elo, form, H2H, referee stats, sentiment.

3. Elo Rating System (25% weight)

Chess-style power ratings with home advantage, goal difference multiplier, and season decay.

4. Sentiment Analysis (10% weight)

Reddit + news sentiment scoring. Weak signal used as a tiebreaker.

Your Weekly Workflow

1. Before each gameweek, hit "Refresh Everything"

2. Check the Value Bets panel -- these are your best opportunities

3. Click into matches that interest you for the deep dive

4. Check if models agree (high confidence) or disagree (be cautious)

5. The Kelly % tells you how much to bet relative to your bankroll

Remember: you WILL lose individual bets. A 40% win rate at good odds is profitable long-term. Trust the process.
