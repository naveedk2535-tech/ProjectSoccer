Model Performance — Help

What is this page?

This is proof the model works. We ran it on 631 historical matches where we already know the results, and measured how well it predicted them.

Accuracy

What % of matches did each model predict correctly. Random guessing would be ~33% (three outcomes: H/D/A). Our ensemble hits 54.4% — significantly better than random.

Brier Score

A more sophisticated accuracy measure. Lower = better. 0 = perfect predictions. 0.25 = random guessing. Our 0.574 is for all 3 outcomes combined (so divide roughly by 3 to compare to single-outcome Brier).

ROI

If you had bet every value bet the model flagged over 3 seasons, what would your return be? +31.3% means for every $100 staked, you'd have $131.30 back.

Calibration

The ultimate trust test. When our model says '60% chance', does that team actually win 60% of the time? Ours shows 60% predicted → 61.9% observed. That's well calibrated — the model knows what it knows.
